My top 3 movies of all the times

Bahubali 2

This movie is multimasalas- Action, drama, romance, thriller. VFX, Music ,set are exceptional. Movie takes glance on the historic lives and quarrels between the cousins.

3 idiots

This is a must watch movie. Movie discoveres story of 3 engineering students who are studying in one of the best colleges in India.It opens how they struggle due to the boundaries set by the educantion system. The genre of the movie is comedy, drama and romance.

Modern love Mumbai

The movie discovers 6 different love strories from Mumbai. Each strory teaches a unique thought. The characters, plot, music is excellent. My favourite scene from the movie is when laali(character) is riding her broken bicycle on the on the flyover, without concerning, living her fullest, each moment believing and loving herself.
